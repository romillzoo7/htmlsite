Rohan's PB and J

by: Rohan Mills

Rohan's PB and J can be enjoyed as an early morning breakfast or a late night snack.

Ingredients

  • Bread
    • nature's own Wheat '100%' whole grain
  • Peanut butter
    • Skippy's peanut butter
  • Starberry Jam
    • Smukers' Jam
  • Butter
    • Country Crock

What to 'do?' Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Apply butter to one bread slice
  3. Apply Strawberry Jam to other bread slice
  4. Apply Peanut butter on top of buttered slice
  5. Put both sides together
  6. Slice the sandwhich diagonally down the middle
  7. Eat and 'Enjoy!'
